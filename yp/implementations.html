YANG-Push Notification, Transport and Capability Discovery and YANG Libary standartization process entered the phase where the specifications are being finalized and validated in implementations.

YANG-Push Publishers:

Cisco IOS XR 25.3.1 (2025-08-31)

Supporting RFC 8639, RFC 8641, draft-netana-netconf-notif-envelope and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-notifications-versioning for YANG-Push configured subscription and IETF-JSON as in RFC 7951 for encoding, draft-ietf-netconf-udp-notif for transport, RFC 9196 and draft-ietf-netconf-yp-transport-capabilities for subscription capability discovery, RFC 8525 and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-library-augmentation to discover YANG modules and its dependencies.
https://software.cisco.com/

Huawei VRP R024C10 for MA5800T-X17 (2025-06-30)

Supporting RFC 8639, RFC 8641, draft-netana-netconf-notif-envelope and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-notifications-versioning for YANG-Push configured subscription and IETF-JSON as in RFC 7951 for encoding, draft-ietf-netconf-udp-notif and draft-ietf-netconf-distributed-notif for transport, RFC 9196 and draft-ietf-netconf-yp-transport-capabilities for subscription capability discovery, RFC 8525 and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-library-augmentation to discover YANG modules and its dependencies.
https://support.huawei.com/

6WIND VSR 3.10

Supporting RFC 8639, RFC 8641, draft-netana-netconf-notif-envelope and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-notifications-versioning for YANG-Push configured subscription and IETF-JSON as in RFC 7951 and IETF-CBOR as in RFC 9254 for encoding, draft-ietf-netconf-udp-notif and draft-ietf-netconf-distributed-notif for transport, RFC 8525 and draft-ietf-netconf-yang-library-augmentation to discover YANG modules and its dependencies.
https://www.6wind.com/
